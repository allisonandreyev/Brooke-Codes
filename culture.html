Culture

Ukrainian culture is a rich tapestry of traditions, art, and history, characterized by a deep sense of community, strong family bonds, and a vibrant celebration of both Christian and pagan customs. One of the most significant aspects of Ukrainian culture is its strong emphasis on hospitality and warm-heartedness. Guests are treated with utmost respect, and sharing a meal together is a common way to bond and build relationships.

    The most famous Ukrainian Holidays are :

  1. Easter - Easter is a very important festival in Ukraine. During Easter, decorated easter eggs called pysanky are made.

  2. Christmas is an important Ukrainian festival. Many Ukrainianians are either religious, or culturally orthodox christians. Christmas has great importance to most Ukrainians. Many Ukrainians go on a 40 day fast in preparation for christmas. Or spend hours cooking recipes like borscht ,holubtsi ,and kutia
