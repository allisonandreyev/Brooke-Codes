Language

Ukraine is a Slavic country that has its own language and alphabet relating to that of other languages in the Slavic family, especially Russian. Many like to joke about how Ukranian sounds like "drunk" or "broken" version of Russian, but in reality it is its own beautiful language, and such comments are considered disrespectful by Ukranians.
